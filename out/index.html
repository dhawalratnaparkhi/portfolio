About Me

I'm a graphic designer who helps business owners, coaches, and consultants turn their LinkedIn into a client magnet.

My specialty? Creating scroll-stopping carousels, data-driven infographics, and branded cheatsheets that do more than look good, they position you as the go-to expert in your field and attract your ideal clients while you sleep. I believe great design should work as hard as you do.

That's why every piece I create is built for engagement, designed to get saved and shared, and crafted to give you back 20+ hours per week. No fluff. No cookie-cutter templates. Just strategic, beautiful content that turns your LinkedIn presence into your most powerful business tool.

Ready to elevate your LinkedIn game? Let's talk about how we can transform your content into a client attraction machine.
